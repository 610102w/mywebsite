Sam's User Journey Map

Stage 1: Planning the Trip

Actions: Sam opens their phone and uses a public transport app to plan the trip to the new gallery. They double-check the bus and subway connections, trying to figure out how much time the journey will take.

Thoughts & Feelings: Sam feels anxious about traveling to an unfamiliar part of the city but is reassured by the transport app's guidance.

Goals: To find a reliable and efficient route.

Pain Points: Uncertainty about the walking distance between the subway and gallery.

Stage 2: Leaving the House

Actions: Sam checks the transport app one last time before leaving. They pack essentials and leave with enough buffer time for potential delays.

Thoughts & Feelings: Excited but also nervous about catching the bus on time.

Goals: To leave early enough to avoid rushing.

Pain Points: Worried about any unexpected delays on the way to the bus stop.

Stage 3: Taking the Bus

Actions: On the bus, Sam keeps an eye on the app and checks the real-time map to ensure they are on the correct route.

Thoughts & Feelings: Focused and slightly nervous about the transfer to the subway.

Goals: To reach the subway station in time for the next transfer.

Pain Points: Difficulty hearing the bus stop announcements and concerns about potential traffic delays.

Stage 4: Subway Transfer

Actions: Sam gets off the bus and enters the subway station. They check their phone again to ensure they are heading toward the right platform. After purchasing a subway ticket, they head toward the platform and wait for the train.

Thoughts & Feelings: Sam feels relieved after successfully making the bus-to-subway transfer but remains nervous about the next stage of the journey.

Goals: To get on the correct subway train, ensuring they are traveling in the right direction.

Pain Points: Sam finds the subway station a bit crowded, adding to their uncertainty about whether they’re on the right platform.
